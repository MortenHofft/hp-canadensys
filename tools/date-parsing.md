Use this tool to parse dates into their component parts. Type or paste dates on separate lines, optionally preceded by your own identifier followed by a tab or a pipe. 

**Example input** 
Jun 13, 2008 
15 Jan 2011 
2009 IV 02 
2 VII 1986 
 
1 | 1999/02/24 
2 | 02/17/1921
